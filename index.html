Welcome

People who are interested in history and ancient Egypt and want to have a deeper understanding of ancient Egypt. There are people who have cats and who love cats, or people who want to adopt cats. Or those who want to raise cats but conditions do not allow.

The resort also provides a lot of family fun activities, especially for children, so that school-age children can experience close contact with cats. Parents can also treat the resort as a family leisure place. For adults, we also provide healing cat yoga for those who want to relax. We also provide volunteer activities to people who want to make a contribution to stray animals come to participate. Special events will also be provided from time to time.
